ABOUT ME

Who are you?

Hey there! My name is Kenneth Poh.

Born and raised in sunny Singapore. I'm currently a second year student under Republic Polytechnic's School of Infocomm persuing a diploma in Digital Design and Development.

Driven and diligent, I try my best to achieve results that I can be proud of no matter what.

Despite learning both design and development, my field of interest lies more within the realm of development. The sense of accomplishment from developing solutions to solve problems is what keeps me going.

In my free time I enjoy playing video games, with my favourite genres being FPS and RPG.

What are your skills?

Through my diploma, I have learnt skills for both design and development. I would say that I lean more towards development, with the breakdown being 2/3 developer and 1/3 designer. The sense of accomplishment from developing solutions to solve problems is what keeps me going: from creating solutions from scratch, to finally fixing a bug that has been hindering progress.
